About the Game

"We're Ducked" is an immersive adventure game where you navigate through challenging environments as adorable low-poly ducks. Face the elements, solve puzzles, and work together to survive in this unique gaming experience.

  • ✓ Beautiful low-poly graphics
  • ✓ Engaging puzzle mechanics
  • ✓ Atmospheric environments
